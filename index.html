LOCAL MAFIA STRIKES FEAR IN THE STREETS: THE RISE OF RAPHAEL

I was born into a world that didn’t give second chances. My father disappeared when I was a kid, and my mother worked tirelessly to keep us alive...

By 17, I joined the O’Driscolls, a ruthless local Mafia. Their boss, Tommy, was calm and calculating... I started with small jobs, earning Respect for being always around but never noticed.

One night, a deal went wrong. Chaos erupted, shots fired... I stayed calm, grabbed Tommy, and got him out before anyone could react. That night, I proved myself.

Years passed, and I rose through the O’Driscolls. I became colder, more focused, understanding that loyalty and patience were everything.

In the O’Driscolls, you either rise... or disappear. Tommy decides which one. Me? I’m still standing. Still watching. Still waiting.
